The Best Roast Potatos

Description

These roast potatos are crunchy, golden and bound to wow any of your dinner guests.

For best results, prep the day before cooking.

Serves 6 people comfortably

30 mins to prep, 1 hr to cook

Ingredients

  • 1kg of baby potatos
  • 4tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1tsp cooking salt
  • 1/4tsp black pepper
  • 5 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 5 sprigs of thyme, or 3 spigs of rosemary
  • finely chipped fresh parsley or thyme to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C / 400°F (180°C fan)
  2. Par boil new potatos in salted water for 10 to 15 minutes
  3. Heat olive oil in pan, in the oven until hot
  4. Toss potatos, garlic and herbs into hot oil
  5. Cook for 20 minutes, check and turn the potatos
  6. Cook undisturbed for the remaining 40 minutes, or until the desired colour
  7. Serve with fresh herb of your choice and enjoy
